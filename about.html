Our Story

JOAGO’s beginnings stem from a Spanish watch company we used to work at. It is here where we realized our chance to make a difference in the watch industry. Manufacturing a cost-effective watch that saves our ocean in the progress, is the mindset we have embodied.


Our products are in demand but we lack the funds. While other companies focus on profit margins, we strive to shift that focus to our valued customers providing them with affordable high-quality watches.


The twenty-first century must become the Century of the Environment, we can no longer afford to use a natural resource only one time. One of our main problems is the ocean, we have been producing and not reusing plastic since its origins and that is enough.


Our mission is to show the world that recycled materials can lead to a great product and move from destructive consumption to a 0 waste system.


We want to create a full line of different types and styled watches that stay in contact with our world and stay at an affordable cost. We will send out several updates throughout the entire process making sure our backers are up-to-date on any important information.


At the beginning, we will work to create a non-profit organization in Indonesia. With the plastic we pick up from the ocean, we would like to recycle our own straps, making the watch almost 100% recycled. The metal that we use for all of our watches has been recycled already, we even have a full recycled steel watch already, but We Want To Get Way Further!


Let’s save our planet because if it’s not us who will?
