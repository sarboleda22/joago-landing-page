Our Story

JOAGO was cultivated from the experience the founders obtained at a Spanish watch company. The founders realized the significant need to reduce marine plastic pollution and the opportunity to improve the oceans and sea life. JOAGO’s cost-effective, eco-friendly watch, made entirely from recycled materials, was subsequently formed. We can no longer afford to use a resource just once; the 21stcentury must become the century of the environment.


Our products are in demand, but we lack the funds. While other companies focus on their bottom line, JOAGO prioritizes ocean sustainability efforts by providing our valued customers with eco-friendly, high-quality watches at an affordable price. The recycled materials in the JOAGO watches primarily include silicon, steel, and nylon. These materials are collected from land and beaches, recycled, and then shipped to our manufacturer who makes these beautiful, JOAGO watches.


Using the funds from this campaign we aim to set up a non-profit organization in Indonesia that collects and recycles ocean waste. We aim to make a watch out of fully recycled ocean waste that we collected from the ocean. We want to create a line of different types and styled watches that stay in contact with our world and stay at an affordable price. We will send out several updates throughout the entire process making sure our backers are up-to-date on any important information and new releases.


Using the funds from the Kickstarter campaign, the founders will establish a non-profit organization in Indonesia focused on collecting and recycling ocean waste. JOAGO’s goal is to create a line of affordable watches made entirely from fully recycled ocean waste The JOAGO team will provide periodic updates to ensure our backers remain informed on any relevant JOAGO updates or new releases.


Let’s save our planet, because if it’s not us, who will?
