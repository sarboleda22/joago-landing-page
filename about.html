Our Story

JOAGO’s beginnings stem from a Spanish watch company we used to work at. A large problem society is facing at the moment is the amount of waste in our oceans, we have been producing and not reusing plastic and other materials for too long. We realized our chance to make a difference in the watch industry. Manufacturing a cost-effective watch that saves our ocean in the progress, is the mindset we have embodied. The twenty-first century must become the century of the environment, we can no longer afford to use a resource only one time.


Our products are in demand but we lack the funds. While other companies focus on profit margins, strive to shift that focus to our valued customers providing them with affordable high-quality watches. The materials used in the watches including; silicon, steel, and nylon, are collected from land and beaches, recycled and then shipped to our manufacturer who makes this beautiful watches.


Using the funds from this campaign we aim to set up a non-profit organization in Indonesia that collects and recycles ocean waste. We aim to make a watch out of fully recycled ocean waste that we collected from the ocean. We want to create a line of different types and styled watches that stay in contact with our world and stay at an affordable price. We will send out several updates throughout the entire process making sure our backers are up-to-date on any important information and new releases.


Our mission is to show the world that recycled materials can lead to a great product and move from destructive consumption toward a zero-waste system.


Let’s save our planet because if it’s not us who will?
